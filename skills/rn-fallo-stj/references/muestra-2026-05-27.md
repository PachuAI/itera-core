## Batch-Level Lessons

- STJ extractos need richer content than lower-court operational decisions, but the first sentence must still be procedural-result accurate.
- The most important classification field is `alcance_decision`.
- A queue/REF/admissibility decision can contain extensive facts; do not let those facts overpower the actual dispositive result.
- Health amparos need separate markers for urgent medical orders and patrimonial disputes.
- Abstract constitutional actions should remain valuable to the institutional index even without merits.
- Execution decisions require cautious language and explicit review-scope limits.
